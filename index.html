Account Deletion Steps

Follow these simple steps to delete your account:

  1. Go to your Profile.
  2. Click on Edit profile.
  3. At the bottom, select Delete Profile.

Data Deletion Policy

When you delete your account, the following data will be removed from our records:

  • Profile information
  • Photos and posts
  • Messages
  • Preferences and settings

No data will be retained once your account is deleted.

Contact our support team for any questions regarding our data safety policies.
